Bureau of Land Management Idaho Range Improvement Program

The BLM supports a series of goals via its range improvement program, which is administered on publicly owned land.

Goals

  • Land health / ecosystem restoration
  • Sustainable livestock grazing
  • Wildlife habitat support
  • Water resource management
  • Cultural/recreational uses
